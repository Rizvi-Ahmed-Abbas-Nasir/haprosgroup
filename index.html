About Us

Nature's Best, Delivered Worldwide

Welcome to Hapros Global, a family-owned, 5th-generation farmer-led organization. Since 1899, we have remained steadfast through changing times, bringing the finest agricultural products to customers around the globe. Our legacy is built on trust, quality, and integrity, ensuring that every partnership we forge reflects commitment to excellence and the well-being of the communities we serve.

About Us

125 Years and Counting

In 1899, Ganpat Pasu, a young man from the Kutch region, left his humble beginnings and ventured to Mumbai, where he took a bold risk in agricultural trading, unknowingly laying the foundation for a 125-year legacy. His son, Khimji, joined the business at just 17, but after his untimely death, the responsibility fell on Ganpat Pasu’s 12-year-old grandson, Khushalchand, who navigated the challenges of post-independence India with remarkable resilience. By 1988, Khushalchand rebranded the company as Khushalchand Khimji & Co., positioning it to thrive in India’s rapidly changing economy. Today, the 5th generation of the family leads Hapros Global, a company that continues to honor its heritage while embracing innovation, ensuring a bright future built on a rich tradition of trust and perseverance.

Sourcing Nature, Supplying Quality

At Hapros, we are cutting the middle-man by providing an end-to-end solution that ensures quality and reliability throughout the entire supply chain

Sourcing

Our sourcing process is anchored in direct relationships with farmers ensuring a transparent and ethical supply chain. By working closely with our network of over 10,000 farmers, we guarantee the quality of each product, from farm to table.

Manufacturing & Processing

Our IAF-recognized & HACCP-certified facilities are equipped to handle a diverse range of agricultural products, from spices and pulses to botanicals and superfoods. With rigorous quality control measures, we adhere to global standards to deliver safe, high-quality products to our clients worldwide.

Trading

Our extensive trading network connects us to over 20 countries, enabling us to offer competitive pricing and reliable delivery channels. As a global supplier, Hapros ensures consistent supply with the highest standards of integrity and professionalism
