Hapros and Animal Welfare

At Hapros Global, our dedication to animal welfare extends beyond business operations into active support for 27 out of 69 Panjrapoles (animal sanctuaries) across India. Rooted in the Jain philosophy of Ahimsa (non-cruelty and non-violence), these shelters serve as sanctuaries for vulnerable animals, preserving a tradition of kindness while building a sustainable future.
Our Role and Impact

• Sanctuaries for Discarded Animals: Panjrapoles provide a safe haven for animals that are no longer economically viable, such as aging bulls, non-lactating cows, or stray animals. These sanctuaries shield them from the meat industry and offer care, nutrition, and security. • Preserving a Historic Institution Since their inception in 1834 under British rule, Panjrapoles have embodied community care for animals. Today, there are 69 Panjrapoles across India, collectively sheltering over 15,521 animals. Hapros’ active management ensures these sanctuaries continue to thrive, blending modern standards of care with their original mission.

• Operational Excellence:
• Hapros contributes through regular funding and strategic improvements, such as: ◦ Enhancing infrastructure to create clean, humane facilities. ◦ Ensuring systematic health monitoring and veterinary care. ◦ Supplying resources like nutritious feed and clean water.

• Promoting Ethical and Humane Practices: By supporting Panjrapoles, Hapros prevents abandoned animals from facing cruelty. Our work inspires local communities to adopt compassionate practices, reinforcing non-violence as a core societal value.
• Empowering through Transparency:

• Impact in Numbers:
• 69 Panjrapoles across India. • 35,896 animals currently sheltered and cared for. • Countless lives saved through rescue efforts and community awareness programs.

Hapros Global’s unwavering commitment to animal welfare reflects our belief in sustainability, compassion, and respect for all living beings. By preserving and enhancing the Panjrapole tradition, we aim to foster a culture of kindness and ethical responsibility for generations to come
