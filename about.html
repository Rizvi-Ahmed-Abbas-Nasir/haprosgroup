Who We are

WHO ARE WE?

Hapros is an evolutionary venture from the house of Khushalchand Khimji & Co. With over 125 years of experience and expertise, we are a sourcing & manufacturing company for high-quality Conventional, IPM and Organic Beans, Spices, Cereals, Botanicals, Herbs, Oilseeds, Dried Fruits and other Superfoods. 

From bulk shipments to custom orders, our trusted and reliable delivery ensures you receive the products you need and promised quality, right on time. Our extensive network and robust supply chain enable us to offer customized solutions and reliable service across India and globally. 

Our ability to predict market trends and manage supply chains efficiently sets us apart as leaders in the food industry.

WHY CHOOSE HAPROS?

At Hapros, we take pride in our relationships with farmers, labourers, some of whom have been with us for over 50 years. This continuity is a testament to our commitment to quality and reliability, ensuring that your business benefits from the same level of excellence that has defined Hapros for over a century.

We procure products from the most remote corners of India and deliver to your warehouse anywhere in the world. We leverage our extensive distribution capabilities, supply chain, and access to a wide network of warehousing and cold storage facilities across India.

Whether it's sourcing raw materials, processing them to your exact specifications, or packing and delivering them in time under optimal conditions, Hapros ensures that every aspect of your procurement is handled with the utmost care and expertise.

Our Promise

Hapros was built on the idea of bridging the gap between farmers in India and buyers across the world, between trust and reliability, in the global supply chain. We understand the challenges you face in sourcing the right products, promised quality at the right price, and we are here to make that process as smooth and transparent as possible.

With our deep industry knowledge, extensive network, and unwavering dedication to quality, we act as an extension of your procurement team, ensuring that your needs are met with precision and care.

When you choose Hapros, you are choosing a partner with over a century of experience, a legacy of trust, and a commitment to excellence. Let us handle the complexities of sourcing and logistics, so you can focus on growing your business with confidence.
