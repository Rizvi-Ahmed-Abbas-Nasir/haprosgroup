Our Heritage

1899

Founding of Ganpat Pasu & Co.

Our journey began in 1899 India, during a time of significant changes and rapid industrialisation. Initially named Ganpat Pasu & Co., the Group was established under colonial rule and successfully navigated the challenges of A rapidly changing world.

About Ganpat Pasu – The man (1899-1920) Ganpat Pasu, a man of humble origins from the Kutch region of Gujarat, travelled to Mumbai, a bustling metropolis that promised opportunity but offered no guarantees. In the face of an unpredictable economic and political landscape, Ganpat Pasu's bold decision to enter the agricultural trading business laid the foundation for what would become a legacy. His foresight and spirit allowed him to establish the business that not only survived but thrived through the decades.

About Khimji Ganpat (1920-1950)

As the business started to grow, Ganpat Pasu brought his eldest son Khimji Ganpat into the fold. At the age of 17, Khimji joined his father and their combined efforts began to shape the Group’s future.

Unfortunately, Khimji Ganpat passed away at the prime age of 35 times.

1950s

1970s

About Khushalchand Khimji (1950-1988)

Following the untimely passing of Khimji Ganpat, the responsibility of the family business fell on his elder son, Khushalchand Khimji. At just 12 years of age, Khushalchand was called upon by his grandfather to join the business and complete his education in Mumbai.

Despite his age, Khushalchand proved to be a sharp and capable young man., Under his leadership, the business expanded into new markets, introduced new products, and forged strong partnerships. A composed man, Khushalchand was instrumental in building a network that connected farmers, millers, suppliers, brokers, freight forwarders, warehousing facilities, labourers, and others in the supply chain.

The Formation of Khushalchand Khimji & Co. (1988)

After his grandfather's passing, Khushalchand rebranded the company as Khushalchand Khimji & Co. in 1988. While the business evolved, the core values, ethics, and principles that Khushalchand had embraced throughout his life remained unchanged, continuing to guide the company’s philosophy and operations.

This rebranding came at a pivotal moment, as India was on the cusp of economic transformation. The liberalization of the Indian economy in 1991 opened new opportunities and markets, and Khushalchand Khimji & Co was well-positioned to seize them.

1970s

The Two Brothers (1988-2024)

As the Khushalchand established the new brand, his two sons, Hemant Shah and Sanjiv Shah, joined the company. They brought with them a modern perspective, essential for navigating the rapidly changing economic and geopolitical landscape. Under the tutelage of Khushalchand, together the brothers helped steer the company to new heights, continuing the legacy of trust, loyalty, inclusivity. The business continued to blossom as the fifth-generation steps in, with a vigour to continue the legacy.

1980s

2024

Evolving for the Future (2024) - Honouring Tradition, Embracing Change

As we arrive in 2024, Hapros Global is launched as a proud 5th generation enterprise, deeply rooted in the legacy that has been carefully built over 125 years. Our journey from humble traders and processors to prominent exporters has been fuelled by an unwavering commitment to our clients and the partners who have stood by us through the decades.

Hapros Global is more than just a company; it is a testament to our enduring values, our ability to adapt, innovate, and lead in the ever-evolving world of international trading. As we move forward, we remain dedicated to carrying this legacy into the future and honouring the past while embracing the opportunities that lie ahead.
